Visionaries Behind RAAS

Founders

RAAS is shaped by two complementary journeys, one rooted in mindful spiritual guidance and one in large-scale cultural curation. Together, this vision blends devotion, music and community into a luxury immersive format built for modern audiences.

Spiritual Life Coach | Meditation Guide | Author

Shweta Rhea Chopra

Shweta Rhea Chopra is a spiritual life coach, meditation guide and author focused on inner awareness and emotional wellbeing. With over a decade of work across children and adults, she has helped many people reconnect with calm, clarity and purpose.

She is the author of As If by Magic, a reflective journal built around self awareness, gratitude and conscious living through simple daily practices. At RAAS, she leads a guided meditation that invites audiences to pause, breathe and return inward.

Her vision for RAAS is to grow a movement that blends music, mindfulness and shared celebration across India and globally, helping people reconnect with peace and resilience through collective spiritual experience.

"Living consciously in self awareness and practising resilience is the most powerful takeaway from any spiritual journey."

Business Leader | Cultural Curator | Hospitality Visionary

Niharika Malhotra

Niharika Malhotra brings business leadership, artistic sensibility and spiritual depth to the creation of RAAS. She is associated with Oasis Group, one of India's largest ethanol suppliers, and has contributed to major brand associations, including IPL ecosystem partnerships.

She has also led hospitality and cultural initiatives, including the upgrade of Oasis Mussoorie - A Member of Radisson Individuals. Her work across entertainment and creative collaborations shaped her approach to building emotionally meaningful community experiences.

Her vision is to expand RAAS as a cultural and spiritual music movement across India and internationally, creating immersive gatherings where devotion, celebration and inner calm can coexist in one powerful shared space.

"RAAS is my attempt to bring together two worlds I deeply believe in - the power of music and the power of faith."
